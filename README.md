PROBLEM STATEMENT
A retail company sells electronic products such as laptops, mobiles, headphones, and
keyboards through stores located in different cities like Pune, Mumbai, and Nagpur.
Every purchase made by a customer is recorded in a sales dataset containing order
details, product information, quantity sold, and price.
Recently, the management noticed fluctuations in sales but they do not know which
products are driving revenue or which cities are performing better. The company wants
to analyze historical sales data to answer important business questions such as:
1. Which products generate the most revenue?
2. Which cities have the highest sales?
3. Which product categories perform best?
4. What are the trends in sales across time?
